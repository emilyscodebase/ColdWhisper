ColdWhisper - My Hackathon Journey

I'm a passionate tech enthusiast and a student at AltSchool Africa, driven by the desire to create meaningful digital solutions. My most notable project so far is ColdWhisper, an AI - powered app that is being designed to support the mental and emotional well-being of developers. Competing with this innovation at the Q-Hackathon 2025, I proudly emerged 4th place, a milestone that strengthened my resolve to merge technology with real world impact.

ColdWhisper was born from my passion for mental health and my curiosity to merge it with technology. The app will detect frustration through voice or facial expressions and offers calming support or actionable tips to help developers regain focus. Building and ColdWhisper during the hackathon taught me resilience, teamwork, and the importance of solving real-world problems with AI.
